About




Christmas is one of the happiest festivals in the
world, and Santa Claus is, without doubt, the most
significant part of the charm.

Santa Claus is known by many other names like Kris
Kringle, Father Christmas, Saint Nicholas, St.
Nick, Kris Kringle, Pelznickel, and more. Santa is
a character who originated in early Christian
culture.

It is believed that Santa Claus brings gifts to
everyone on Christmas Eve along with candies and
toys. He has a sleigh and his famous reindeer that
pull the sleigh all across the world to deliver
presents.
